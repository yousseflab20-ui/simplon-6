About Us

Surf Taghazout is a local project born from the heart of the ocean in Taghazout, a small village world-famous for its waves and beautiful beaches We are a group of young surfers brought together by our love for the sea and surfing, and we want to share this passion with everyone — whether youre trying surfing for the very first time or looking to improve your skills. Our goal is to build a community that respects the ocean, nature, and local culture. We organize lessons, surf trips, and meet-ups where people can connect, learn, and enjoy the adventure. Surf Taghazout is not just a surf school or a tourist activity — its a small family open to anyone who loves the ocean and the waves.

Meteo

The weather plays an important role in our daily lives. It influences the clothes we wear, the activities we do, and even our mood. Sunny days make people feel energetic and happy, while rainy days often bring a calm atmosphere. For surfers, the weather is even more important, because wind, waves, and temperature decide whether the conditions are good or not. Knowing the weather forecast helps everyone plan better and stay safe.
